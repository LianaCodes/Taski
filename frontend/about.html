About Taski

Taski is a calm study planner designed to help students manage classes, tasks, exams, and focused study sessions. Built with gentle design and practical defaults, Taski reduces friction so planning becomes a habit.

Our mission

We believe planning shouldn't be stressful. Taski helps students form consistent habits and make progress without noise. We prioritize clarity, gentle visuals, and small features that remove friction from everyday study.

Key features

  • Classes: Organize courses, attach assignments and exams, and keep course notes in one place.
  • Tasks: Quick-add tasks with due dates and priorities.
  • Study sessions: Built-in timers and Pomodoro support to help you focus. Session notes are attached to tasks or classes.
  • Notes & Calendar: Write quick notes within app or import from google docs,plus a calendar view to visualize your semester timeline.
  • Integrations: Planned Google Classroom import to pull courses and assignments (opt-in).

Roadmap

Planned near-term work includes: Google Classroom import, richer calendar syncing (ICS/Google), improved task automation (smart due-date suggestions), and a mobile-friendly responsive overhaul.
